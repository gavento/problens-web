# What This Mini-Course Covers <a id="what-this-course-covers"></a>

We take a problem-driven approach in this mini-course. We begin with real-world puzzles and challenges [at the beginning of it](00-introduction), and use them to understand KL divergence and the related theory.

Throughout the mini-course, we will explore concepts like KL divergence, entropy, cross-entropy, maximum likelihood and maximum entropy principles, machine-learning loss functions, mutual information, and more. The applications of this are in the areas of probability, statistics, machine learning, information and coding theory, financial math, and more.

By the end of this course, you should understand not just how KL divergence and related techniques works but, hopefully, you should have a better language and intuitions for all kinds of problems that involve reasoning under uncertainty.
